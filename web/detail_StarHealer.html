Dr. Star Healer

Pediatric Oncology — “Guiding every child to shine again.”

About

Dr. Star Healer brings hope and brightness to every treatment room. Her gentle humor and care make difficult therapies feel less scary and more empowering for children and their families.

Department

The Pediatric Oncology Department helps young patients fight and recover from cancer with advanced therapies, psychological support, and family education.

Fun Facts for Kids

  • Dr. Star Healer’s lab coat sparkles under UV light!
  • Stars you see in the sky are millions of miles away — but Dr. Star Healer says every brave kid shines just as bright.
  • Her favorite snack is star-shaped fruit salad.

Positive Energy Tips
