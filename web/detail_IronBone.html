About Iron Bone

Equal parts coach and inventor, Iron Bone helps kids recover from fractures, surgeries, and sports injuries. He designs strength quests that feel like game levels and teaches families how to spot safe progress at home.

Fracture recovery Adaptive sports training Custom braces & casts

Strength Forge Studio

The orthopedic studio houses physiotherapy, gait analysis, and casting experts under one ceiling. Iron Bone collaborates with biomechanic engineers to tailor mobility gadgets and home routines for every hero in rehab.
